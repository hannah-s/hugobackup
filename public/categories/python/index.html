A couple of weeks ago, I’ve enrolled to Introduction to Computer Science and Programming Using Python, commonly referred to as MITx 6.00.1x. The concept behind EDX is fairly simple: you get to pick from a vast catalogue of courses, offered by the most renowned universities in the world, and audit them for free.
